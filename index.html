Pancakes

  • Time: 10 min.
  • Serves: 3
Ingredients Quantity
eggs 2
sugar 2 tbl spoons
oil 2 tbl spoons
milk 4 tbl spoons
flour 200g
water 400ml
salt a little bit

Step 1: Mix everything except flour and water.

Step 2: Pour flour and mix the dough.

Step 3: Start to pour water slowly.

Step 4: Cook pancakes in the pan.

Source: Beatos lietiniai

Tomato soup

  • Time: 120 min.
  • Serves: 5
Ingredients Quantity
onions 2
carrots 2
garlic 1
spices how much you like
pickled tomatoes 800g
broth 1l
mushrooms 10

Step 1: Cook shredded onions, carrots, put salt and peppers.

Step 2:Slice mushrooms and garlic, cook.

Step 3:Slice mushrooms and garlic, cook. Pour broth and spices, boil for 15 minutes.

Step 3:Put pickled tomatoes, mash the soup.

Source:Beata's tomato soup
